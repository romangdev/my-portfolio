My Story, in a Nutshell...

For over 5 years, I freelanced and consulted as a copywriter. And while it was was interesting at times, it truly wasn't fulfilling to me. The products sold weren't really valuable , and the work itself wasn't too stimulating. This lead to my eventual pivot to development. With family and friends full of software developers, and having "dabbled" in programming myself for years with much excitement, I decided to fully commit to transitioning to web development. And I must say, I'm extremely happy with the decision. I'm learning new things every day, and am constantly being challenged to come up with interesting solutions that actually help people. Currently, I'm focused on front end dev (although I've played on the back end as well), and am picking up ReactJS.
